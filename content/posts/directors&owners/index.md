## Profit Leakage Ka Real Source

Most mills me losses dramatic nahi hote.  
Wo quietly leak hote rehte hain:

- Breakdown ke time temperature loss  
- Repair ke time material waste  
- Idle running ke time power wastage  
- Wrong manpower deployment se inefficiency  

Individually small lagta hai.  
Collectively profit kha jaata hai.
